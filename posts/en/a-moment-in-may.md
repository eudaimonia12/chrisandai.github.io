On the fourth day of the May Day holiday I was strolling along West Nanjing Road when a small garden at the center of the street caught my eye. Its entrance was covered in Chinese roses and rambling roses, and scattered petals of every color floated on the surface of the water, dreamlike. I badly wanted to photograph the scene, but couldn't find the right angle. Suddenly I noticed a man and a woman crouched side by side, seemingly puzzling over how to take a good picture themselves. And so I caught, in that instant, the shapes of the two of them.
